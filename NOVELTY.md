## STDM Topological Framework: Applied Operational Core

This framework is a specialized signal processing engine designed to analyze raw transit baseband streams and deep-space telemetry. The pipeline operates as a deterministic event-trigger and RFI mitigation suite for high-end astrophysics:

* **Cosmic Interceptions:** Detects and captures non-stationary "special signals" and anisotropic wave-packet shifts caused by hard-particle collisions and high-energy cosmic rays.
* **Terrestrial Noise Excision (The Lying Detector):** Automatically isolates and zeroes out local instrumentation artifacts, including 50 Hz/60 Hz PSU ripples, ADC clock leaks, and computer bus interference.
* **Cross-Platform Telemetry Mapping:** Natively ingests and cross-calibrates raw CHIME terrestrial telescope logs and NASA Cassini planetary archival datasets (`.TAB`/`.LBL`).
